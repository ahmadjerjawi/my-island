# Apple Tree Tile

## Overview[]  
  
_The Apple tree tile tiles are epic tile, you can get them via rotating
shop.**When placed won't make you any money per second**. Apple tree tile are
regrowable type of tile which lets you collect planted fruits for money. Every
collect theres a timer which blocks you from collecting them too quickly. The
timer is 30 min per collect._

When tropical tree tile is placed you need to acquire following items; **5
Stem[![](wiki_images/25)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/my-island-
solstice/images/3/34/Stem.png/revision/latest?cb=20250711203615) and 35 Wild
Seeds** [![](wiki_images/25)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/my-island-
solstice/images/2/2e/Seed.png/revision/latest?cb=20250626192658). After you
collected those items you can build the tree.

# Farmland Tile

## Overview[]  
  
the farmland tile is a non-breakable tile that costs 1000$ gives $1/s income
(only when the construction is done)

when you place down a farmland, it requires
[![](wiki_images/21)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/my-island-
solstice/images/0/0b/Rock.png/revision/latest?cb=20250626111349)10 Rocks and
[![](wiki_images/26)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/my-island-
solstice/images/2/2e/Seed.png/revision/latest?cb=20250626192658)10 Wild seeds
in order to be built, and in order to plant a crop on the farmland, you need
to stand on it while having Wild seeds in your inventory, it takes 1 wild seed
to plant any crop

after a crop is planted, each crop will take a certain amount of time to grow,
the grow time can be skipped through spending robux (carrots take 29
[![](wiki_images/19)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/my-island-
solstice/images/4/41/Robux.png/revision/latest?cb=20250625101843) while all
other crops take 79
[![](wiki_images/yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/my-
island-solstice/images/4/41/Robux.png/revision/latest?cb=20250625101843))

after the crop is done growing it can be harvested to receive money, it gets
directly converted into money rather than being sent into your inventory to be
sold, the yield can be viewed on the bottom left corner of the screen and its
chosen from within a different range for each crop

## Overview[]

Gamepasses provide permanent gameplay benefits and can be purchased with Robux
[![](wiki_images/20)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/my-island-
solstice/images/f/f2/Robux_gold.svg/revision/latest?cb=20250720115135),
ranging in price from 25
[![](wiki_images/20)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/my-island-
solstice/images/f/f2/Robux_gold.svg/revision/latest?cb=20250720115135) to 399
[![](wiki_images/20)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/my-island-
solstice/images/f/f2/Robux_gold.svg/revision/latest?cb=20250720115135). These
passes are designed to enhance the overall quality-of-life experience in the
game, rather than offering overpowered advantages.

In addition to Gamepasses, the in-game Robux Shop offers a variety of optional
purchases, including bundles, microtransaction and global or server-wide
events that can be activated for Robux
[![](wiki_images/yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/my-
island-solstice/images/f/f2/Robux_gold.svg/revision/latest?cb=20250720115135)
to boost gameplay for all players on the server/game.

## Section Header

### yield

250 wood/hit

### hits

10

### income

$50/s

Prismatic tree is a **limited** unique tree tile obtained by completing
[Trevor's](/wiki/Trevor "Trevor") quests, every hit you gain
**250[![](wiki_images/27)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/my-island-
solstice/images/d/df/Wood.png/revision/latest?cb=20250625113713)** and can it
can be hit **10** times before needing regrowth

Prismatic tree requires no construction once placed and gives **$50/s** income

In order to obtain Prismatic Tree, you must complete 3 quests given by trevor,
**the quests are not always the same,** once you claim all 3 quests, a 6 hour
timer starts before you can complete them again and receieve another Prismatic
tree

# Rocks Tile

## Overview[]  
  
_The rocks tile are rare tiles, costing_ **750$** _each and making**0$/s.**
Gold Rock variants you get chance to gain Gold from while collecting stone
from the tile. Same goes to Diamond Rock variants, you get chance to gain
Diamond while gathering stone from the Diamond Tile! The chance for both of
the materials is 10%_

_The Rotating Shop is the main place to buy new items in My Island. Its
inventory changes regularly, offering a limited-time selection of buildings,
decorations, and utilities. Players need to check back often, as some items
may only appear for a short period. Managing your resources and shopping
strategically is key to expanding and customizing your island effectively. The
tile shop refreshes every 3 minutes._

**If you want to know more about the tiles, click on highlighted name in order
to get to know more about certain tiles!** _But don't forget some of the
page's are still yet to be made, this wiki is being updated ever 1-2 days from
the update. Be patient we're working on this site at our own pace!_

The Rotating Shop is the main place to obtain most of the tiles currently
available in the game. Each tile offers a unique style and function-some need
to be built, while others are ready to use right away. Keep in mind that not
all tiles are fully finished-some are still being improved and updated.

## Informations

### Name

Grudge

### Arrive

45 Minutes

### Departs

15 Minutes

  
The Traveling Merchant is a unique game mechanic that spawns a shop on the
central island, allowing you to buy exclusive tiles and helpful items to
support your overall progress.

Grudge arrives every 45 minutes. The timer starts when the Traveling Merchant
departs or when a new server is created. This event is server-specific, not
global. Once she appears, you have 15 minutes to buy whatever you want before
she leaves.

Traveling merchant includes few unique items/tiles which can be helpful in
later stages of the game. She contains things like: Tile Mover, [Mesa
Tile](/wiki/Mesa "Mesa"), [Terracotta Tile](/wiki/Terracotta_Tile "Terracotta
Tile"), [Castle Tile](/wiki/Castle_Tile "Castle Tile") and Spirit Blossom
Tile! You can find more information in the Items List section.
